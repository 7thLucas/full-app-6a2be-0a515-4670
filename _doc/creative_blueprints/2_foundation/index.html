The Big Picture

Problem & Solution

The Problem

Car owners juggle scattered tools — and leave affiliate revenue on the table.

  • ✕

    Maintenance tracked on paper or forgotten — missed service intervals lead to costly repairs.

  • ✕

    Fuel costs logged separately with no link to vehicle health data or spending trends.

  • ✕

    High-intent car accessory shoppers exist inside the app — but there's no store to convert them, and commission income is zero.

The Solution

One Flutter app. Three modules. Passive commission on every sale.

  • ✓

    Maintenance module — digital service log with reminders, history, and multi-vehicle support.

  • ✓

    Fuel tracking module — fill-up logs, efficiency analytics, and monthly spend charts.

  • ✓

    Affiliate shop module — curated car accessories via affiliate links; earn 3–8% commission on every user purchase, no inventory required.

Why It Converts

Users who track oil changes already trust the app with their car. The shop turns that trust into revenue — no ad spend, no cold traffic.
